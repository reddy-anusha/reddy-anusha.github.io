About

Android App

For my capstone project, my group and I created an android app for students, to enhance their time management and study skills. It has 5 features: flashcards, calendar, notes, pomodoro timer, and white noise; which were developed using the agile method. The goal of this app is to be a one-stop destination for students. For example, the calendar tab allows students to combine their work and school calendars.

Link to the project on github.
